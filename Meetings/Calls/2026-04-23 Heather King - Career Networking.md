## Key Advice Shared (applicable to John too)

### LinkedIn Strategy
- Connect within **48 hours** of every conversation — simple message: *"Great to meet/talk, would love to stay connected"*
- Use LinkedIn to find 1st and 2nd connections at target companies → request introductions
- Include specific job link + LinkedIn profiles of connections when asking for intros
- Set profile to **"Open to Opportunities"** for recruiter visibility

### Networking Process (informational interview structure)
1. Express flexibility and openness to different paths
2. Share background and types of roles targeting
3. Ask for insights and advice (use 25 of 30 minutes for this)
4. Ask: *"Keep me in mind for opportunities you hear about"*
5. Ask: *"Anyone else you'd recommend I speak with?"*
- Follow up every **2 weeks** if no response — be comfortable being persistent

### Two Career Paths in Finance
1. **Structured programs at major banks** — formal hiring (e.g. 20 analysts/year), rigorous training, excellent mentors, high standards
2. **Earlier-stage companies** — more immediate responsibility, less structured mentoring, greater autonomy

**Recommendation:** Prioritise working with excellent people for first role — training quality matters more than title or responsibility level.

### Tracking
- Maintain a **spreadsheet tracking all contacts and conversations**
