## Key Finding

At 20-day horizon, Q4 (positive surprise) + Q1 (low velocity) earns the highest returns (+0.671%). The velocity interaction runs counter to H4's prediction — low velocity amplifies PEAD for positive surprises, high velocity suppresses it. This reversal is the core of the H4 non-support finding.
