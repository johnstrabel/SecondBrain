# Mukund Harsha & Kesava Rao (2024) — Exploring Profitable Opportunities: Analysing Technical Indicators Combinations for Profitable Trading

**Full citation:** Mukund Harsha, A. & Kesava Rao, V.V.S. (2024). Exploring profitable opportunities: Analysing technical indicators combinations for profitable trading. *Corporate & Business Strategy Review*, 5(1), 148–160.

## Summary

Studies combinations of three technical indicators — SMA (Simple Moving Average), OBV (On-Balance Volume), and CCI (Commodity Channel Index) — across 47 combinations tested on 1-year data, with top-5 combinations validated on 10-year data for 100 companies. Finds that indicator combinations outperform individual indicators; specific combinations vary by market regime.

## Key Arguments

1. **Three indicator types:** SMA = trend (lagging), OBV = volume-based (leading), CCI = momentum (leading) — complementary signal types.
2. **47 combinations tested:** Exhaustive search across sub-combinations and parameter settings.
3. **10-year validation:** Top-5 combinations confirmed on longer history for 100 companies — robustness test.
4. **Combination synergy:** Leads + lagging indicators together produce more reliable signals than either alone.
5. **CCI as momentum indicator:** Measures current price relative to its average — detects overbought/oversold conditions.

## Relevance to Thesis

- Example of the "combining signals" approach at the technical analysis level — motivates the thesis's multi-signal hierarchy
- OBV and price-based features correspond to log_volume and log_price features in the thesis's ML model
