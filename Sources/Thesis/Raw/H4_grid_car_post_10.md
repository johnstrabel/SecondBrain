# H4_grid_car_post_10.csv — H4 Interaction Grid: CAR[+1,+10] by Surprise × Velocity Quartiles

## What This File Contains

4×4 grid of mean CAR[+1,+10] for each surprise × velocity quartile cell.

| Surprise Q | Velocity Q1 | Velocity Q2 | Velocity Q3 | Velocity Q4 |
|------------|------------|------------|------------|------------|

| Q4 (pos) | +0.0018 | +0.0035 | +0.0018 | +0.0024 |

## Key Finding

The 10-day window shows more consistent directional patterns for extreme surprises. Q4 (positive) is positive throughout (0.0018–0.0035); Q1 (negative) is mostly negative. However, there is no monotonic relationship with velocity — Q2 velocity shows the highest 10-day CAR for positive surprises (0.0035), not Q4 velocity.
