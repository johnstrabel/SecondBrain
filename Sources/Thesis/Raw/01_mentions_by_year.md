# Chart 01: Reddit Mentions by Year (2010–2023)

## What This Chart Shows

Bar chart of total Reddit stock ticker mentions per year across all 15 subreddits, 2010–2023. Generated by `analyze_data.py`.

## Expected Content

- X-axis: Year (2010–2023)
- Y-axis: Total mention count
- Key feature: Sharp spike in 2020–2021 (WallStreetBets/GME meme stock phenomenon)
- Generally increasing trend from 2010 baseline (~low thousands) to 2021 peak (~millions)
- Slight decline post-2021 as meme stock frenzy subsided

## Key Insights

- The 2021 spike dominates the dataset — GME, AMC, and other meme stocks dramatically inflated total counts
- Long-run growth reflects increasing retail investor engagement on Reddit
- Pre-2015 coverage is sparse — Reddit finance communities were smaller then
- Motivates the thesis's use of the 7-day/90-day velocity ratio (normalizes for the secular trend)

## Data Source

2.74M Reddit mentions from 15 subreddits (WallStreetBets, investing, stocks, etc.) collected via Arctic Shift API.
