# Buinevici (2019) — Rivals as Allies: Combining Fundamental and Technical Analysis for Stock Investing

**Full citation:** Buinevici, I. (2019). Rivals as Allies: Combining Fundamental and Technical Analysis for Stock Investing. Bachelor thesis, Charles University, Faculty of Social Sciences, Institute of Economic Studies.

## Summary

Tests four investment strategies on European stock data: (1) momentum, (2) momentum + BOS ratio filter, (3) fundamental buy-and-hold using F-SCORE, (4) combined strategy (all three). The combined strategy generates statistically significant monthly risk-adjusted return of 0.938% (1-month rebalancing); this holds for 3, 6, and 9-month holding periods. Combined strategy significantly outperforms all individual strategies.

## Key Arguments

1. **Combined strategy dominates:** Fama-French three-factor alpha of ~0.938%/month for the combined strategy.
2. **F-SCORE as fundamental filter:** Piotroski's F-SCORE applied to screen European stocks — fundamental quality filter.
3. **BOS ratio:** Price-based momentum metric combined with F-SCORE to filter momentum signals.
4. **Synergy confirmed:** Combining fundamental and TA signals is more powerful than either alone — directly supports information fusion thesis.
5. **European data:** Unlike US-focused studies; tests generalizability of combined approaches.
